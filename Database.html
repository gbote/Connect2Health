About Us

Who are we?

Connect2Health is an organization that aims to provide information to the general public about healthcare access in their area. In this day and age, healthcare should never be something that is hard to come by whether it be a medical checkup or a hospital visit. At Connect2Health, we make it our goal for people to receive healthcare with as little access as possible.

What do we do?

We help people from all backgrounds access healthcare through informational means such as the website you are on right now! We provide maps, and databases that people to know information about healthcare providers before they get there.

Why do we do it?

We do it because we believe that healthcare should not be hard to obtain. It's a human right that should not have any bars to its access. In this day and age, everything is for profit, and because of that many bars still exist in the way of healthcare. Because of this, Connect2Health in order to alleviate these difficulties.
